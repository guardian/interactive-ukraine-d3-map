The Pandora papers detail over 1,500 UK properties held through 716 offshore companies. Most are concentrated in London, chiefly within just two boroughs: Westminster and Kensington.

The offshore companies involved can be traced to a variety of jurisdictions including Jersey, the Cook Islands and Seychelles. However most of the companies are registered in the British Virgin Islands, a UK overseas territory. The tax haven hosts 678 of the 716 companies revealed by the Pandora papers.

The offshore company owners exposed by the leaked data come from every continent, with 78 different nationalities represented. Around 10% are Nigerian, about 7% come from India.
